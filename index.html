Membership Tiers

Choose how you want to be part of the herd.

Each tier gives a different level of connection to Serenity Lake Farm. Start with simple farm days, or go all-in with glamping nights and legacy-level perks.

Tier A – Farm Friend
For locals who want regular farm days.

Perfect for nearby families who love hanging with the cows, wandering the property, and unplugging for a few hours at a time.

  • ✓ Farm day visits for your household (with reservation)
  • ✓ Discounted Highland cow encounters & pasture experiences
  • ✓ Invites to member-only bonfires & seasonal events
  • ✓ Early access to workshops, photo days & holiday happenings
  • ✓ Member email updates about the herd and new happenings
Apply for Tier A

Best for: local families, grandparents, and folks who want farm days more than overnights.

Tier B – Wyldstay Weekender
Your go-to escape for glamping + cows.

Built for families and couples who want overnight stays baked right into the membership, plus full access to the farm rhythm.

  • ✓ Glamping nights at Wyldstay included each year
  • ✓ Priority booking for weekends, school breaks & special dates
  • ✓ Farm day visits for your household (with reservation)
  • ✓ Extra stay perks: s’mores kits, firewood bundles & Cat-style surprises
  • ✓ Member pricing on photo sessions, dinners & mini-retreats
Apply for Tier B

Best for: out-of-town families, tired parents, and couples who crave a regular reset.

Tier C – Founding Herd Member
For the superfans who want deeper roots here.

A legacy-level membership for people who want Serenity Lake Farm to be “their” place in a bigger way—for family, friends, or even their brand.

  • ✓ Additional glamping nights to spread across the year
  • ✓ Guest passes so friends, clients or collaborators can join you
  • ✓ Priority access to new builds (living pool, cabins & future stays)
  • ✓ One private “bring your people” farm gathering per year
  • ✓ A legacy touch on the land (like naming a tree or calf in the Founding Herd)

How it works

Joining the Serenity herd is simple.

Memberships are intentionally limited, so the farm always feels calm, never crowded. Here’s how we bring new humans into the herd:

1

Choose your tier

Pick Tier A, B or C based on how often you want to visit and whether you want glamping nights built into your year.

2

Submit your application

Fill out the quick form (linked in each tier) so Cat can learn about your family, your lifestyle, and how you hope to use the farm.

3

Confirm & book your first visit

Once your membership is confirmed and pricing shared, you’ll receive member-only links to book farm days, glamping nights, and experiences.
